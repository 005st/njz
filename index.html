NewJeans (Korean: 뉴진스; RR: Nyujinseu), also known as NJZ (엔제이지; Enjeiji), is a South Korean girl group formed by ADOR, a small-label of Hybe.

The group is composed of five members:

  • Minji (Kim Min-ji)
  • Hanni (Pham Hanni)
  • Danielle (Danielle Marsh)
  • Haerin (Kang Hae-rin)
  • Hyein (Lee Hye-in)

The group debuted on July 22nd, with the song Attention.
